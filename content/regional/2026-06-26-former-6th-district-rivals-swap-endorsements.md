# Former 6th District Rivals Swap Endorsements

**TL;DR:** Two former congressional candidates for Virginia's 6th District—Beth Macy and Tom Perriello—have announced mutual endorsements.

Beth Macy and Tom Perriello, who previously ran against each other for Virginia's 6th Congressional District seat, have announced they are endorsing each other. This political alignment represents a shift in the race, which includes portions of Chesterfield County. The endorsement swap signals strategic cooperation between two prominent candidates in the district.

**Why it matters:** This endorsement from prominent political figures could influence how Chesterfield residents in the 6th District perceive the campaign and their representation heading into the election.
