# Virginia Legislature Races to Finalize State Budget

**TL;DR:** Virginia lawmakers meet Monday to finalize the state budget before a June 30 deadline to avoid a government shutdown.

The Virginia House of Delegates and State Senate will meet Monday in Richmond to resolve remaining disagreements over the state budget. Lawmakers face a June 30 deadline, after which a failure to agree could trigger a government shutdown. Both chambers adjusted their schedules to prioritize reaching a final budget agreement.

**Why it matters:** A state budget shutdown could disrupt funding for schools, public safety, and other county services that Chesterfield residents rely on.
