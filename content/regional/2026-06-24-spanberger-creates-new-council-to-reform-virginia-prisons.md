# Spanberger Creates New Council to Reform Virginia Prisons

**TL;DR:** Governor Spanberger launched a new council to address longstanding problems in Virginia's prison system by bringing together advocates, faith leaders, healthcare professionals, and public safety officials.

Gov. Abigail Spanberger announced the Governor's Community Partnership Council On Corrections this week to tackle persistent issues within Virginia's prisons. The council brings together representatives from advocacy groups, faith-based organizations, healthcare providers, and public safety agencies to develop solutions to challenges that have concerned residents and correctional officers for years.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents who work in corrections, have family members in the system, or support criminal justice reform will be affected by the council's recommendations and any resulting policy changes.
