# Virginia budget deal clears hurdle amid statewide policy shifts

**TL;DR:** Virginia state leadership agrees on a budget framework while felons face voter registration delays and localities push back on data center expansion.

State budget leaders have reached a preliminary agreement on a spending deal. Meanwhile, some formerly incarcerated Virginians eligible to vote this summer are caught in registration limbo. In separate regional developments, Amherst County residents are urging supervisors to restrict data centers, and Virginia tribes are organizing to protect the historical site of Chief Powhatan's birthplace in Henrico County.

**Why it matters:** State budget decisions affect funding for local schools, infrastructure, and services in Chesterfield; voter registration policy changes can impact turnout in county elections.
