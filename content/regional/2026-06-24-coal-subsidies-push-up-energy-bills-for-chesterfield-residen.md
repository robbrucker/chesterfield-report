# Coal Subsidies Push Up Energy Bills for Chesterfield Residents

**TL;DR:** Trump administration coal subsidies are raising electricity costs for Chesterfield households without meaningfully benefiting the coal industry.

The Trump administration's latest coal industry subsidies are expected to increase energy costs for Virginians, including Chesterfield County residents, without providing significant economic benefits to the coal sector. While the subsidies appeal to the president's political base, the coal industry faces structural challenges these subsidies don't address. Local households and businesses may see the costs reflected in rising utility bills.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents could face higher electricity costs as federal coal subsidies get passed through utility bills, directly affecting household budgets and local business expenses.
