# Virginia budget approves teacher raises, reshapes data center taxes

**TL;DR:** Virginia's new state budget includes teacher pay increases and changes tax incentives for data center operations.

The Virginia General Assembly has approved a state budget that increases compensation for teachers across the state, responding to ongoing educator pay concerns. The budget also modifies tax policies for data center operations, a significant sector for economic development in many Virginia regions. Both changes carry direct implications for Chesterfield County's schools and local business climate.

**Why it matters:** Teacher raises could help Chesterfield schools recruit and keep experienced educators, while data center tax modifications may shape future business investment and economic development in the county.
