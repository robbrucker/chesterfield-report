Some weeks the county feels small in the best way. This was one of them.

Ten days ago, two Chesterfield officers were shot and a K-9 named Knight was killed. It was the kind of news that stops you in the driveway. What happened next told you something about this place. A local restaurant turned its dining room into a fundraiser for the wounded officers and hung a photo of Knight by the door. People showed up. That is the part the police scanner never captures.

The same week, Matoaca's softball team won a state title on a walk-off, a new trail broke ground out at Winterpock, and the Planning Commission signed off on bringing Southside Speedway back to life. Grief and a ball game and a groundbreaking, all sharing a Tuesday. That is a county, not a headline.

If you read nothing else below, read the civic calendar. Early voting for the August primary starts June 18, and the people who show up are the ones who decide how the rest of this gets written.

See you on the porch.
