# Suspect arrested in Chesterfield bank robbery

**TL;DR:** Keith Patterson, 49, was arrested on Laprade Street after a robbery at C&F Bank in Chesterfield on June 29, 2026.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** Keith Patterson, age 49
- **What:** Robbery at C&F Bank
- **When:** June 29, 2026, around 11:30 a.m.
- **Where:** C&F Bank, 4800 block of W. Hundred Road, Chesterfield, Virginia

## The story

Chesterfield County Police responded to a robbery call at C&F Bank in the 4800 block of W. Hundred Road around 11:30 a.m. on June 29. Using descriptions provided by bank employees, officers identified and apprehended a suspect.

Keith Patterson, 49, was arrested in connection with the incident. According to police, Patterson was located on Laprade Street and taken into custody without further incident.

Specific details about what transpired during the robbery, the amount taken, and whether anyone was injured remain limited at this time. The investigation continues, and authorities are urging anyone with additional information to contact Chesterfield Police.
