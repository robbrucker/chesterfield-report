# McGuire Faces Primary Challenge in Reelection Bid

**TL;DR:** Rep. John McGuire enters his first reelection campaign for Virginia's 5th Congressional District facing criticism over constituent service and recent redistricting changes.

U.S. Rep. John McGuire, a Goochland Republican representing Virginia's 5th Congressional District, is running for reelection for the first time. His campaign faces turbulence, including accusations that he has done little for constituents. A recent court decision invalidated a voter-approved redistricting amendment that Democrats had hoped would favor their party in the district.

**Why it matters:** The outcome of McGuire's primary race affects whether constituents in the 5th District—which includes portions of Chesterfield—will have a new congressional representative.
