# State Restores Funding for HIV Healthcare Programs

**TL;DR:** Virginia's General Assembly restored funding for HIV treatment and support services, including the Ryan White program and opioid recovery initiatives.

The General Assembly has restored funding for HIV healthcare programs, including the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program, which provides education, care, and support to people living with HIV. The restoration also supports workforce development programs and opioid recovery services. The funding reversal addresses critical gaps in community health services.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents with HIV and those in recovery from opioid use disorder depend on these healthcare and support programs to access treatment and rebuilding resources in their community.
