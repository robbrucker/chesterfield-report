# Virginia passes budget with teacher raises, data center tax updates

**TL;DR:** The Virginia General Assembly approved a state budget that includes raises for teachers and changes to data center taxation.

The Virginia General Assembly has finalized a state budget that provides teacher salary increases and modifies tax treatment for data centers. The budget reflects legislative priorities around education funding and economic development incentives. These changes will take effect with the new fiscal year.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield schools may see additional funding support for teacher compensation, while the data center tax changes could affect local business investment and the county's tax base.
