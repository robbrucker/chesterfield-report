# Man charged for false shooting report at local 7-Eleven

**TL;DR:** A Chesterfield County resident was charged with making a false report of a shooting near a 7-Eleven store.

A man has been charged after falsely reporting a shooting incident near a 7-Eleven in Chesterfield County. The false report prompted an emergency response from law enforcement. No shooting actually occurred.

**Why it matters:** Making false emergency reports wastes police resources and puts responders at risk, diverting attention from actual public safety needs in the community.
