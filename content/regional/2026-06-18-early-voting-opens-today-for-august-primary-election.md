# Early voting opens today for August primary election

**TL;DR:** Chesterfield voters can cast early ballots starting today for the August 4 primary elections.

Early voting for the August 4 primary elections is now available to Chesterfield voters. The primaries will determine which candidates advance to the general election across multiple races. Residents can vote in person during the early voting period before Election Day on August 4.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents who cannot vote on primary day now have the opportunity to cast ballots early, and the races decided in the primaries will shape the November general election ballot.
