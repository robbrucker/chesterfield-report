# Virginia Authorizes AI Cameras for Traffic Enforcement

**TL;DR:** Virginia has approved a new law allowing municipalities to deploy AI-powered cameras that automatically catch drivers running stop signs or failing to yield at crosswalks.

A new Virginia law enables local governments to install artificial intelligence cameras to automatically enforce traffic violations at stop signs and crosswalks. The technology represents an expansion beyond traditional police enforcement. Chesterfield County officials will eventually decide whether to adopt this capability in their community.

**Why it matters:** If Chesterfield deploys these cameras, the technology could increase enforcement of traffic laws designed to protect pedestrians while potentially generating revenue for the county.
