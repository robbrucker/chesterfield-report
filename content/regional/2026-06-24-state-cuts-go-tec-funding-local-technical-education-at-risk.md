# State Cuts GO TEC Funding; Local Technical Education at Risk

**TL;DR:** Virginia's state budget eliminates funding for GO TEC, a technical education program that serves schools across the state.

Virginia's General Assembly has dropped funding for the GO TEC program in its recently passed budget. The program provides hands-on technical education to students in 49 school divisions across the state. The cut comes as employers increasingly struggle to find workers with technical skills.

**Why it matters:** If Chesterfield schools participate in GO TEC, the loss of state funding could reduce technical education opportunities for local students and worsen the regional shortage of skilled workers in technical fields.
