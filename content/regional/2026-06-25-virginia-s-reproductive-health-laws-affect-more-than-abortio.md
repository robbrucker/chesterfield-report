# Virginia's Reproductive Health Laws Affect More Than Abortion

**TL;DR:** Virginia's reproductive health laws have broader impacts on pregnancy care and medical decisions than many residents realize, according to patients and healthcare providers.

Virginia's reproductive health laws create real complications for patients navigating pregnancy care, including in-vitro fertilization and miscarriage management. Healthcare providers and patients say these regulations affect medical decision-making in ways that extend well beyond abortion policy. The laws can impact how and where patients receive care during pregnancy loss and other reproductive healthcare needs.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents should understand how state reproductive health laws may affect their own healthcare options and medical decisions, particularly around pregnancy, miscarriage, and fertility treatment.
