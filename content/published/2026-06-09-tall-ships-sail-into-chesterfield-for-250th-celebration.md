# Tall Ships Sail Into Chesterfield for 250th Celebration

**TL;DR:** Chesterfield is hosting events to mark America's 250th anniversary, with tall ships arriving this Thursday.

Chesterfield County is launching its celebration of the nation's 250th anniversary with several upcoming events. Tall ships will arrive in the county this Thursday as part of the festivities. Additional celebration details are expected to be announced.

**Why it matters:** The bicentennial celebration brings community events and tourism opportunities to Chesterfield, offering residents a chance to mark this national milestone locally.
