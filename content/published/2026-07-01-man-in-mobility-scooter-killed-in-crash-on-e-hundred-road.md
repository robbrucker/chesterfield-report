# Man in Mobility Scooter Killed in Crash on E. Hundred Road

**TL;DR:** A man operating a mobility scooter was struck from behind and killed on E. Hundred Road in Chesterfield County early Wednesday morning, July 1, 2026.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** Adult male on mobility scooter (name withheld pending family notification)
- **What:** Struck from behind by a vehicle while operating mobility scooter
- **When:** Approximately 5:40 a.m., Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Where:** 2800 block of E. Hundred Road, Chesterfield County, Virginia

## The story

A fatal crash claimed the life of a man operating a mobility scooter on E. Hundred Road in Chesterfield County early Wednesday morning. According to Chesterfield Police, the scooter was being operated in the westbound lane of the road when it was struck from behind by another vehicle at approximately 5:40 a.m. The victim was pronounced dead at the scene. The driver of the vehicle that struck the scooter is cooperating with authorities as the investigation continues.

E. Hundred Road was closed for several hours following the incident, affecting traffic from Allied Road to the bridge into Hopewell as authorities investigated the crash. Police have not released the identity of the victim pending family notification, nor have they disclosed details about the vehicle or its driver at this stage of the investigation.

This incident highlights ongoing safety concerns for vulnerable road users in the county. Residents with information about the crash are encouraged to contact Chesterfield Police directly.
