# Virginia roundup: data center debate, gun safety bill signed

**TL;DR:** Statewide news includes Democratic tensions over data centers, tribal advocacy in Henrico County, new gun safety legislation, and planned AI camera deployments at traffic stops.

Virginia's data center expansion is creating friction among Democratic lawmakers, while tribal nations are mobilizing to protect historical sites in Henrico County. Governor Spanberger signed gun safety legislation this week. The state is also rolling out AI cameras at stop signs, though details on implementation and scope remain limited.

**Why it matters:** These statewide policy decisions—on infrastructure, safety legislation, and traffic enforcement—may eventually affect Chesterfield residents through state budget priorities, law enforcement practices, and future development patterns.
