# Virginia adopts new framework for solar projects on farmland

**TL;DR:** Virginia has passed a new law establishing rules for solar energy development on agricultural land.

Virginia has enacted legislation to regulate solar energy projects on farms across the state. The law creates a framework for how solar installations can be developed on agricultural property. The measure aims to balance renewable energy expansion with preservation of farmland resources.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield County's agricultural landowners may be affected by new state rules governing solar development options on their property, and residents should understand how this law shapes future land use in the county.
