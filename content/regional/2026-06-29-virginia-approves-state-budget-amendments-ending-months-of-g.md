# Virginia approves state budget amendments, ending months of gridlock

**TL;DR:** The Virginia General Assembly approved Gov. Spanberger's budget amendments Monday, resolving a months-long dispute just before the new fiscal year begins.

The Virginia General Assembly approved a package of budget amendments proposed by Gov. Abigail Spanberger on Monday, formally ending a monthslong budget fight among the Democratic majority. The vote came with less than 48 hours remaining before the start of Virginia's new fiscal year, and both the House of Delegates and state Senate supported the amendments.

**Why it matters:** The state budget determines funding for schools, public safety, roads, and services that affect every Chesterfield resident's daily life.
