# Southside technical education program faces funding cliff

**TL;DR:** A middle school engineering program in Southside Virginia is at risk of ending without continued state funding.

GO TEC introduces middle school students to hands-on engineering and technical careers, and has become important to Southside Virginia's economic development efforts. Without state funding, the program may end this year. Regional supporters warn the loss would damage the pipeline of young people entering technical trades.

**Why it matters:** The uncertain future of regional workforce development programs highlights funding pressures affecting Virginia's technical education infrastructure and workforce readiness across Southside communities.
