# Abortion ruling's effect on miscarriage care remains unclear

**TL;DR:** Four years after the Dobbs decision overturned abortion rights, confusion persists over how doctors should manage miscarriages under new restrictions.

A Virginia Mercury investigation found that the 2022 Dobbs decision and resulting state abortion restrictions have created ongoing uncertainty in how doctors treat miscarriages. Medical professionals across the state continue to struggle with interpreting the laws, leaving pregnant people unsure about their care options when complications arise.

**Why it matters:** Virginia residents facing miscarriage face delays and confusion in receiving time-sensitive medical care due to unclear state law, directly affecting healthcare access in Chesterfield County.
