# Virginia approves $28.5 billion six-year transportation plan

**TL;DR:** Virginia's transportation board has approved a $28.5 billion spending plan for infrastructure over the next six years.

Virginia's transportation board has greenlit a $28.5 billion budget to fund road, transit, and infrastructure projects across the state over six years. The plan represents a significant investment in the state's transportation network. Details on which specific projects Chesterfield will benefit from have not yet been announced.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents may see improvements to local roads and transportation infrastructure as funding from this plan is allocated to county projects in coming years.
