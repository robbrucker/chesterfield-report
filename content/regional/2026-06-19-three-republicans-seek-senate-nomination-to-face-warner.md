# Three Republicans seek Senate nomination to face Warner

**TL;DR:** Three Republican candidates are competing in the Aug. 4 primary, each citing ties to Trump, to earn the chance to challenge U.S. Senator Mark Warner.

Three Republican candidates are competing in Virginia's Aug. 4 primary election for the party's U.S. Senate nomination. Each candidate is highlighting connections to former President Donald Trump. Early voting is already underway, with the winner advancing to face incumbent Senator Mark Warner in the November general election.

**Why it matters:** The race will determine who represents Chesterfield residents in the U.S. Senate on major national issues over the next six years.
