# Teen hurt in March Chester shooting incident

**TL;DR:** A teenager was injured by gunfire on Cosmic Road in Chester on March 15, 2026, and police are investigating the incident.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** A teenage victim; suspect fled the scene
- **What:** Shooting incident; teenager sustained a gunshot wound
- **When:** Sunday, March 15, 2026, at approximately 3:16 p.m.
- **Where:** 6400 block of Cosmic Road, Chester

## The story

On Sunday, March 15, 2026, Chesterfield County Police responded to a shooting on the 6400 block of Cosmic Road in Chester. Officers found a teenage victim in a parking lot who had sustained a single gunshot wound. The teen was transported to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries and was expected to recover.

The suspect fled the scene on foot and was described as a male wearing a dark-colored sweatshirt. Police stated the incident appeared to be isolated and posed no ongoing threat to the community. The motivation for the shooting and circumstances leading to the incident have not been disclosed as the investigation remains active.

Chesterfield County Police have asked anyone with information about the shooting to contact the department at (804) 748-1251, Crime Solvers at (804) 748-0660, or through the P3 app. The police department emphasized the importance of community assistance in solving the case.
