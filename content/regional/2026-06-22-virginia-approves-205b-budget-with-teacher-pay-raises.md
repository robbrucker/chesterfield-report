# Virginia Approves $205B Budget with Teacher Pay Raises

**TL;DR:** Virginia lawmakers passed a two-year budget with 4% raises for teachers and increased education funding.

Virginia legislators have approved a $205 billion two-year state budget that includes a 4% pay raise for teachers and increased funding for public education. The budget also funds healthcare expansion, state employee raises, and new revenue sources including a retail marijuana marketplace and a tax on data centers. The spending plan aims to maintain state services despite expectations of reduced federal funding.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield County teachers will receive state-mandated pay increases, and the county's schools will benefit from the additional education funding approved in this budget.
