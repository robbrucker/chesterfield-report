# Virginia Senate Backs Data Center Tax Exemption, Adds Generator Tax

**TL;DR:** Virginia's Senate budget plan preserves a major sales tax break for data center companies while introducing a new tax on diesel generators.

The Virginia Senate released a revised budget proposal that preserves the longstanding sales and use tax exemption for data centers, resolving a key dispute between legislative chambers. The plan introduces a tiered tax targeting diesel generators used in the industry, expected to generate $1.8 billion in new revenue. The compromise seeks to balance tax incentives that attract data center investment against the state's need for additional revenue.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield County hosts major data center operations from major tech companies, making this state tax policy a direct factor in the local business climate and the county's ability to compete for future tech investment.
