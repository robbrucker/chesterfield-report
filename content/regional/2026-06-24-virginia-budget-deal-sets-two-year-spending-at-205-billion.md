# Virginia Budget Deal Sets Two-Year Spending at $205 Billion

**TL;DR:** Virginia lawmakers approved a $205 billion budget to fund state services over the next two years.

The Virginia General Assembly has approved a $205 billion budget that will guide state spending for the next two fiscal years. The budget funds core state services including education, transportation, and public safety. As a result, Chesterfield County schools and local government services receive state funding allocations determined by this statewide spending plan.

**Why it matters:** The state budget directly affects funding for Chesterfield schools, roads, and public services that county residents depend on.
