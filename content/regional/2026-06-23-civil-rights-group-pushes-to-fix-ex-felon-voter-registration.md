# Civil Rights Group Pushes to Fix Ex-Felon Voter Registration

**TL;DR:** A civil rights organization filed a court motion seeking faster action to resolve delays preventing thousands of Virginians with past felony convictions from registering to vote.

Thousands of Virginians with certain past felony convictions remain stuck in voting registration limbo despite a civil rights victory earlier this year. A civil rights group filed a motion on June 18 asking the court to speed up reforms to the voter registration process. The group accused election officials of ignoring a voting rights lawsuit they had already won.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents with past felony convictions may be directly affected by these registration delays and have a stake in how quickly Virginia resolves the issue.
