# Virginia Legislature Approves Budget, Averts Shutdown

**TL;DR:** Virginia's state budget has been approved, preventing a partial government shutdown.

The Virginia General Assembly passed a state budget in time to avoid a partial government shutdown. The approval came days before the deadline when government operations would have been disrupted. State funding for services and operations will continue uninterrupted.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents depend on state funding for schools, transportation, and public services—a shutdown could have delayed or reduced those resources.
