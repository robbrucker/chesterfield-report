# Offshore Wind Could Save Virginia Millions During Storms

**TL;DR:** A new Virginia offshore wind project could have prevented $10 million in energy costs during Winter Storm Elliott if it had been online at the time.

Virginians faced significant costs importing expensive natural gas during Winter Storm Elliott in 2022. The new Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind project could have supplied domestic wind energy instead, potentially saving the state roughly $10 million in a single day. Experts believe offshore wind will strengthen Virginia's energy independence and stability during severe weather events.

**Why it matters:** Cheaper, more reliable energy from offshore wind could reduce utility costs and grid vulnerability for Chesterfield residents.
