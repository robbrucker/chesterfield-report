# Lawmakers reconvene Monday to review Spanberger budget changes

**TL;DR:** Virginia lawmakers will meet Monday to consider Gov. Spanberger's amendments to the state budget before the new fiscal year begins.

The Virginia General Assembly reconvenes Monday to review proposed amendments to the two-year state spending plan, less than 48 hours before the new fiscal year starts. The House of Delegates will begin at noon. Lawmakers passed the original budget plan just one week earlier, and the governor's amendments will need approval before the deadline.

**Why it matters:** State budget decisions directly affect funding for schools, transportation, and county services that Chesterfield residents depend on.
