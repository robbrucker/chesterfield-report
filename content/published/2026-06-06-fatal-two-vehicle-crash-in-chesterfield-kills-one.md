# Fatal Two-Vehicle Crash in Chesterfield Kills One

**TL;DR:** A passenger in a Honda sedan died Friday night in Chesterfield County after the car collided with a Dodge Ram turning onto Watchrun Drive at the 6400 block of Hopkins Road.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** Female passenger in Honda sedan; drivers and passengers of both vehicles
- **What:** Two-vehicle collision between southbound Honda sedan and Dodge Ram making left turn
- **When:** Friday, June 5, 2026, around 9:20 p.m.
- **Where:** 6400 block of Hopkins Road at Watchrun Drive, Chesterfield County, Virginia

## The story

A fatal two-vehicle crash on Friday night claimed one life on Hopkins Road in Chesterfield County. Around 9:20 p.m., a Honda sedan traveling south on Hopkins Road collided with a Dodge Ram pickup truck that was turning left onto Watchrun Drive in the 6400 block. A female passenger in the sedan was transported to a local hospital where she was pronounced dead from injuries sustained in the crash; her identity is being withheld pending family notification.

Three other occupants were injured in the collision. The drivers of both vehicles and a passenger in the Dodge Ram were transported to area hospitals with non-life-threatening injuries and were expected to recover. Chesterfield County Police Department is continuing to investigate the incident and has asked anyone with information to contact investigators.

The crash occurred as Chesterfield County continues broader efforts to improve traffic safety across the region. In February 2026, the county experienced its first pedestrian fatality of the year on Woods Edge Road. The county has been actively working on road safety initiatives, including a Safe Streets for All grant request approved in 2026 for planning and infrastructure projects totaling nearly $14.1 million to prevent traffic fatalities and serious injuries.

Hopkins Road, a major thoroughfare in the county, has been the subject of prior safety studies. County transportation officials have used Regional Surface Transportation Program funds to study improvements at the Hopkins Road and Chippenham Parkway interchange to improve operations and safety, and speeding has been identified as a concern in the area, particularly near school zones.

**Why it matters:** This fatality marks another loss in Chesterfield County's traffic safety challenges in 2026 and highlights the ongoing risk at intersections throughout the county. The crash serves as a reminder to residents about the consequences of inattention at busy intersections and underscores the need for continued investment in and implementation of safety improvements.
