# Police Search for Missing Girl and Woman

**TL;DR:** Chesterfield Police have issued a CODI Alert for a missing 6-year-old girl and 37-year-old woman last seen in Northern Virginia, with authorities concerned for their safety.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** Thelma Jessie Cockrell, 6, and Jasmine Courtney Cockrell, 37
- **What:** CODI Alert issued for missing child and woman with health and welfare concerns
- **When:** Last seen June 11, 2026 at 8 a.m.
- **Where:** Wolfe Street in Alexandria, Virginia

## The story

Chesterfield Police have issued a Critical Child in Danger (CODI) Alert for 6-year-old Thelma Jessie Cockrell, who was last seen on the morning of June 11, 2026, on Wolfe Street in Alexandria. The child is described as having black hair, brown eyes, standing 4 feet tall and weighing 50 pounds. She was last seen wearing a long white t-shirt with a cartoon character on the front.

Thelma was last seen with 37-year-old Jasmine Courtney Cockrell, who is also missing. Jasmine has black hair, brown eyes, and weighs 115 pounds. She was last seen wearing a blue and purple sundress. Authorities believe the pair may be traveling in a red 2016 Mazda CX5 bearing Virginia license plate SWR-9898.

Police have expressed serious concern for the safety of both individuals due to health and welfare concerns involving Jasmine Cockrell. The CODI Alert system is used by law enforcement to quickly disseminate information about missing children believed to be in danger, activating emergency broadcasts and alerts to the public across Virginia.

Anyone with information about the whereabouts of Thelma Jessie Cockrell or Jasmine Courtney Cockrell is urged to contact the Chesterfield County Police Department immediately at 804-748-1251. Tips can also be reported to the National Center for Missing &amp; Exploited Children.

## Key players

- **Chesterfield County Police Department** — Law enforcement agency coordinating search and issuing alert
- **Virginia State Police** — Assisting with CODI Alert investigation

**Why it matters:** The CODI Alert mobilizes public awareness and law enforcement resources across the region to locate missing children in potential danger. Residents who see the vehicle or individuals matching the descriptions are urged to contact police immediately, as rapid public response can be critical in cases involving missing children.
