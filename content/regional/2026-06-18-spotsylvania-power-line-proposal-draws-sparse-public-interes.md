# Spotsylvania power line proposal draws sparse public interest

**TL;DR:** A planned 115-mile transmission line project held a public hearing in Spotsylvania this week, but few residents showed up despite opposition to similar projects elsewhere.

A proposed 115-mile transmission line that has prompted pushback in other locations held a public open house in Spotsylvania this week. The turnout was notably low. The project's larger footprint and regional scope suggest it may interest residents across the area as it moves forward.

**Why it matters:** Large transmission infrastructure projects can affect multiple counties and impact regional property values, utility rates, and environmental concerns—issues that often cross county lines and merit resident attention.
