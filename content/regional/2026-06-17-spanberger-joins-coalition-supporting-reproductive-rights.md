# Spanberger Joins Coalition Supporting Reproductive Rights

**TL;DR:** Virginia Governor Abigail Spanberger has joined the Reproductive Freedom Alliance, a coalition of governors working to protect abortion access and reproductive healthcare.

Gov. Abigail Spanberger announced this week that Virginia has joined the Reproductive Freedom Alliance, a multistate coalition of governors committed to protecting reproductive rights. The alliance supports legislation and ballot measures while helping member states obtain medications like mifepristone used for abortion and miscarriage management. Spanberger has signed Virginia legislation into law aligned with the coalition's goals.

**Why it matters:** The alliance's work will influence reproductive healthcare access and policy options available to Virginia residents, including those in Chesterfield County.
