# Virginia Law Brings Pay Transparency, Bans Salary Questions

**TL;DR:** New Virginia law requires employers to disclose pay ranges to job applicants and prohibits asking candidates about previous salaries.

A new Virginia law mandates that employers reveal pay information to job applicants and forbids asking about salary history during hiring. The law aims to close pay gaps and level the playing field for workers negotiating compensation. Employers must adapt hiring practices to comply with these requirements.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield job seekers gain bargaining power and transparency, while local employers and government agencies must update hiring policies to meet the new requirements.
