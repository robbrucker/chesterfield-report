# Chesterfield Planning Commission Meets June 16

**TL;DR:** The Chesterfield Planning Commission will meet on June 16, with residents able to attend in person or submit comments online.

The Chesterfield County Planning Commission will convene on June 16. Residents interested in the proceedings can choose to attend the meeting in person or participate remotely by submitting comments through an online form. This dual-option approach ensures community members can engage with the planning process regardless of scheduling constraints.

**Why it matters:** The meeting allows Chesterfield residents to directly influence county planning decisions affecting land use and community development.
