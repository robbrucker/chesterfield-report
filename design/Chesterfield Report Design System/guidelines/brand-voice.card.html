// We write

  • Plain, declarative sentences. The fact first.
  • "The county" — never "officials say" filler.
  • Active voice: Supervisors approved, not was approved.
  • Mono timestamps & numbers as receipts: 19:42 · 4–1 vote.

// We avoid

  • Hype, clickbait, or "you won't believe."
  • Emoji in headlines or body.
  • National-politics framing on local stories.
  • Jargon without a plain-language gloss.
