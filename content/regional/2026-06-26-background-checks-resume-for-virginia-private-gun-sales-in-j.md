# Background checks resume for Virginia private gun sales in July

**TL;DR:** Virginia will require background checks for private firearm sales starting in July.

Virginia will reinstate mandatory background checks for private firearms transactions beginning in July. The state is also addressing other matters: cannabis advocates are challenging a proposed increase to penalties for public consumption, schools in Danville have been released from state corrective action following progress, and Virginia is stocking waterways with walleye and saugeye to support recreational fishing.

**Why it matters:** As Virginia residents, Chesterfield families may be affected by these state policy changes affecting gun sales procedures, education standards, cannabis regulations, and outdoor recreation opportunities.
