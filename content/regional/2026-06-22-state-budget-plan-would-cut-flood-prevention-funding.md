# State budget plan would cut flood prevention funding

**TL;DR:** Virginia's proposed budget would redirect money away from flood prevention and toward other state priorities.

A Virginia state budget proposal would redirect funds from the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative (RGGI) away from flood prevention and climate-related programs. RGGI money has traditionally supported environmental resilience and flood mitigation efforts across the state. The shift would repurpose those dollars for other state budget needs.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents depend on state funding for flood prevention infrastructure and planning; this redirection could reduce available resources for local flood mitigation and emergency preparedness.
