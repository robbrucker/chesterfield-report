# Federal Court Blocks Trump Mail Voting Restrictions

**TL;DR:** A federal judge blocked major parts of President Trump's executive order restricting voting by mail, finding it exceeded his constitutional authority.

A federal judge on Thursday blocked key portions of President Trump's executive order that would have restricted voting by mail, ruling the order exceeded his constitutional authority. The decision temporarily prevents the U.S. Postal Service from requiring states to provide lists of likely mail voters. An appeal is expected.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents who rely on mail voting will continue to have access to this voting method without the restrictions this executive order would have imposed.
