# Old Dominion Power seeks rate increase; residents can weigh in

**TL;DR:** Old Dominion Power has asked regulators for a rate hike, and Virginia residents, including Chesterfield County customers, can submit public comments on the proposal.

Old Dominion Power, which serves Chesterfield County and other parts of Virginia, is requesting approval for a rate increase from state regulators. Residents have an opportunity to voice support or concerns about the proposal through a public comment process. The state is accepting input before making a final decision on whether to grant the utility's request.

**Why it matters:** If approved, the rate increase would raise electricity bills for Chesterfield households and businesses that rely on Old Dominion Power for service.
