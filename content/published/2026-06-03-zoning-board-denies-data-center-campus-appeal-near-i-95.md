# Zoning Board Denies Data Center Campus Appeal Near I-95

**TL;DR:** Chesterfield's Zoning Appeals Board rejected an appeal challenging the approval of a proposed data center campus near Interstate 95.

The Chesterfield Zoning Appeals Board has denied an appeal related to a proposed data center campus planned near I-95. The decision upholds an earlier zoning determination on the commercial development project. The ruling represents the final step in the local appeals process for this proposal.

**Why it matters:** Zoning decisions shape what kinds of development come to Chesterfield and affect land use, traffic patterns, and the county's economic growth.
