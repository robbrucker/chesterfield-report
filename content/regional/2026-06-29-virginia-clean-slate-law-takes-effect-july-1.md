# Virginia Clean Slate law takes effect July 1

**TL;DR:** A new Virginia law allowing certain criminal convictions to be sealed goes into effect July 1, giving residents with qualifying records a fresh start.

Virginia's Clean Slate law takes effect July 1, allowing people to petition the courts to seal certain criminal convictions from their records. The law aims to help residents move forward after serving their sentences by removing barriers to employment, housing, and other opportunities. Courts across the state are preparing for implementation and an expected increase in petition filings.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents with eligible criminal convictions may now petition local courts to seal their records, potentially opening doors to employment, housing, and educational opportunities that had been blocked due to their past convictions.
