# Federal Bill Threatens Virginia Rideshare Safety Rules

**TL;DR:** A federal transportation bill amendment could override Virginia's state-level safety protections for Uber and Lyft users.

A proposed amendment to the federal BUILD America 250 Act could preempt state-level safety regulations for ride-hailing apps. State legislators, including those from neighboring Colorado, are pushing back against the language, warning it would strip away protections that states like Virginia have put in place for passengers and drivers. The amendment was added during committee review by a California House Republican.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents who use Uber or Lyft could lose state-level safety protections if this amendment passes, depending on what Virginia's current rideshare regulations cover.
