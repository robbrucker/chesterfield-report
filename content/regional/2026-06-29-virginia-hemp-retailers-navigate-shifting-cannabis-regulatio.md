# Virginia hemp retailers navigate shifting cannabis regulations

**TL;DR:** As Virginia prepares to launch a legal cannabis market next summer, hemp retailers are reassessing their business strategies amid changing state and federal regulations.

Virginia's legal cannabis market is set to launch next summer pending final state budget approval, creating uncertainty for existing hemp retailers who sell infused products like creams and drinks. Regulatory shifts at both state and federal levels are forcing some business owners to reconsider their operations. Hemp retailers are navigating an evolving legal landscape as the cannabis market formalizes.
