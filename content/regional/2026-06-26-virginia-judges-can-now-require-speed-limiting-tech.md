# Virginia Judges Can Now Require Speed-Limiting Tech

**TL;DR:** Virginia judges gain the authority to require certain drivers to install speed-limiting devices in their vehicles as part of sentencing.

A new Virginia law expands judicial authority to impose penalties on dangerous drivers. Judges can now mandate that some offenders equip their vehicles with technology that restricts how fast they can drive, adding a tool to courts' enforcement options. The requirement would apply to drivers convicted of serious traffic violations or related offenses.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents affected by reckless driving may see faster intervention through the courts, while the technology offers another layer of traffic safety enforcement in the county.
