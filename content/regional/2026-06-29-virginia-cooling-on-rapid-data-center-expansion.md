# Virginia Cooling on Rapid Data Center Expansion

**TL;DR:** Virginia's enthusiasm for rapid data center growth is cooling as officials consider building facilities in locations where they could deliver greater economic benefit.

Virginia, home to the world's largest concentration of data centers, has seen appetite for rapid expansion in congested suburbs diminish. Observers note that rather than opposing data centers outright, the state should be more strategic about siting new facilities. Directing growth to areas where data centers would meet genuine needs could generate stronger economic returns for Virginia communities.

**Why it matters:** State policy on data center placement affects growth patterns, infrastructure demand, and development opportunities across Virginia counties including Chesterfield.
