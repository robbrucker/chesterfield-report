# Chesterfield Honors 44th Annual Senior Volunteer Hall of Fame

**TL;DR:** Chesterfield County celebrated its 44th annual Senior Volunteer Hall of Fame ceremony, recognizing local seniors for outstanding community service.

Chesterfield County held its 44th annual Senior Volunteer Hall of Fame ceremony on June 4 to recognize seniors who have provided unusual or outstanding service to the community. The program celebrates the contributions of local seniors who have strengthened the county through their volunteer work. The recognition underscores how experienced community members play a critical role in advancing the county's quality of life.

**Why it matters:** Honoring senior volunteers acknowledges the vital role experienced residents play in building community strength and can inspire others to contribute their time and talents to Chesterfield.
