# Virginia lawmakers approve state budget with data center tax

**TL;DR:** Virginia's new budget keeps tax incentives for data centers but adds a new tax on their electricity use.

State lawmakers have finalized a budget deal that maintains existing tax breaks for data center companies while introducing a new electricity tax on their operations. The budget also funds an inland port project in Washington County and includes money for a statue of Onzlee Ware in Roanoke. The package reflects a compromise between competing priorities in the state budget.

**Why it matters:** Changes to data center tax policy could affect local business recruitment and state revenue that funds education and services for Virginia residents.
