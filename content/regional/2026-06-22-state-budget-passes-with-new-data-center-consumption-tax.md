# State budget passes with new data center consumption tax

**TL;DR:** Virginia's General Assembly passed a new state budget that includes a consumption tax on data centers.

The Virginia General Assembly has approved the state budget, which introduces a consumption tax on data centers. The spending bill now goes to Gov. Abigail Spanberger, who has the authority to apply line-item vetoes to the legislation.

**Why it matters:** As a major data center hub, Virginia's tax policy on these facilities could affect the region's economic competitiveness and future investment in server infrastructure.
