# Missing 6-Year-Old and Woman Found Safe

**TL;DR:** A 6-year-old girl and a 37-year-old woman who disappeared from Chesterfield County on June 10 were both located safely within 48 hours, with police citing health and welfare concerns as a factor in the missing persons case.

## Quick facts

- **Who:** 6-year-old Thelma Jasmine Cockrell and 37-year-old Jasmine Courtney Cockrell
- **What:** Missing persons case; both located safely after going missing from their home
- **When:** Went missing June 10, 2026 around 1 a.m.; both found safe by June 12
- **Where:** 14000 block of Cherry Street in Chesterfield County; last sighting in Alexandria on June 11

## The story

A 6-year-old girl and a 37-year-old woman disappeared from a home on Cherry Street in Chesterfield County early Wednesday morning, June 10, prompting a search that quickly escalated to a statewide alert. Virginia State Police issued a CODI (Child Observation Declared in Emergency) Alert on Thursday, June 11, for 6-year-old Thelma Jasmine Cockrell. The alert also sought information about 37-year-old Jasmine Courtney Cockrell, who was believed to be with the child. Police expressed concern for their welfare due to existing health and welfare concerns involving the adult.

By Thursday morning, the pair was spotted in Alexandria on Wolfe Street, providing a key lead in the search. The case drew attention from multiple news outlets and law enforcement agencies across the region. On Friday morning, authorities announced that the 6-year-old had been found safe. Search efforts continued for the adult, who remained missing into the weekend.

By Saturday morning, June 12, Chesterfield County Police confirmed that the 37-year-old woman had also been located and was safe. The department did not immediately release details about where either individual was found or the circumstances of their recovery. Police indicated they were continuing efforts to establish contact and carry out further investigation into the circumstances of the case.

The safe recovery of both individuals brought an end to the search effort and appeal for public assistance that had been issued just two days earlier.

## Key players

- **Chesterfield County Police Department:** Coordinated search and issued CODI Alert
- **Virginia State Police:** Issued CODI Alert for missing child

**Why it matters:** The case demonstrates the effectiveness of rapid alert systems like CODI in locating missing children quickly. The safe return of both individuals provides relief to the family and community after a brief period of uncertainty about their welfare.
