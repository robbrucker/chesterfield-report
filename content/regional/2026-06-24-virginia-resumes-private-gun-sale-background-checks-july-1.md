# Virginia Resumes Private Gun Sale Background Checks July 1

**TL;DR:** A Virginia court dissolved an injunction that halted private firearm sale background checks, allowing them to resume July 1 statewide.

A Lynchburg Circuit Court judge ruled in favor of Attorney General Jay Jones' request to dissolve an injunction that had blocked background checks for private firearm sales since October. The decision restores the Virginia State Police's ability to conduct background checks at gun shows and private sales. Checks are set to resume July 1.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield residents who buy or sell firearms privately will again have access to required background check procedures.
