# Virginia explores faster, cheaper bachelor's degree option

**TL;DR:** Virginia is joining an initiative with Ohio to create a three-year bachelor's degree model to help students graduate sooner and save money.

Virginia higher education leaders are partnering with Ohio to develop a three-year bachelor's degree pathway that would cut the traditional four-year timeline. The move aims to align degree programs with what employers need and improve college affordability for students. This follows Virginia's broader strategy to make higher education more accessible and cost-effective.

**Why it matters:** Chesterfield students and families could benefit from lower total education costs and the option to enter the workforce or pursue graduate studies a year sooner.
